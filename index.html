How did COVID-19 impact the US in 2020?

COVID-19 has tremendously affected the US.

We will explore the actual impact by observing the number of cases and the resulting death toll by each state.
We will also study the fatality of COVID-19, which is calculated by the ratio of deaths to cases.

In order to show distinct changes, the situation of March, July and November will be depicted as a chart.
